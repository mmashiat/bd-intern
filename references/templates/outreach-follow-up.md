# Follow-Up Templates

## Standard Follow-Up

**Subject**: Re: {original subject}

{Reference timeframe since last contact — "Following up from our conversation last {day/week}"}

{One new piece of value — new info, resource, insight, or development relevant to them.}

{Restate the ask, slightly reframed if the first attempt got no response.}

## Intro Request

**Subject**: Intro to {person} at {company}?

{Why specifically — what's the connection or angle.}

{Offer: here's a blurb you can forward.}

**Forwardable blurb**: "{2-3 sentences about your company and what you'd like to discuss with the intro target.}"

{Name}

---

## Rules for Follow-Ups

- **<100 words** for standard follow-ups
- Don't repeat the original pitch — add something new
- If second follow-up with no response, change the angle
- After 3 attempts with no response, flag as cold in pipeline
- Reference the specific prior interaction, not generic "following up"
