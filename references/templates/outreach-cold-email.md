## Rules for All Cold Emails

- **<150 words** total
- Subject line: specific, not generic ("Re: your Series B" not "Partnership opportunity")
- Opening: proves homework (reference THEIR product, news, or tech)
- Connection: one sentence linking their situation to your product
- Detail: one concrete thing (metric, capability, integration)
- CTA: one specific ask (15-min call, not "let's connect")
- No buzzwords, no AI tells, no fluff
- Sign with name + email, no long signatures
