# Pipeline Stages

Detailed stage definitions with entry/exit criteria and typical durations.

## Discovery

### 1. Discovery Backlog (0-7 days)
- **Entry**: New lead identified (referral, inbound, research)
- **Exit**: First conversation scheduled or completed
- **Actions**: Research company, prepare outreach, make initial contact
- **Health check**: Should not stay here >7 days. If stale, outreach hasn't happened.

### 2. Discovery Active (7-30 days)
- **Entry**: Initial conversation happened
- **Exit**: Qualified (good fit, mutual interest) or disqualified (not a fit)
- **Actions**: Discovery calls, technical assessment, identify decision makers, understand needs
- **Health check**: Should qualify or disqualify within 30 days. Lingering here = unclear fit.

### 3. Discovery Done (7-14 days)
- **Entry**: Qualified — clear fit, mutual interest, identified product/use case
- **Exit**: Agreement to engage (pilot, POC, partnership)
- **Actions**: Define scope, align on product fit, propose engagement, get buy-in
- **Health check**: Transition to delivery should happen quickly once qualified.

## Delivery

### 4. Delivery Backlog (0-14 days)
- **Entry**: Engagement agreed upon
- **Exit**: Work begins
- **Actions**: Resource allocation, kickoff scheduling, contracts/legal
- **Health check**: Delays here usually mean legal/procurement blockers.

### 5. Delivery Active (30-90 days)
- **Entry**: Work underway (integration, pilot, POC)
- **Exit**: Work complete, ready for review
- **Actions**: Active integration work, weekly check-ins, issue resolution
- **Health check**: Flag if 60+ days with no milestone. Regular updates essential.

### 6. Delivery Review (7-30 days)
- **Entry**: Work complete, under review
- **Exit**: Approved for launch
- **Actions**: Testing, legal review, compliance checks, sign-offs
- **Health check**: Delays here = legal/compliance blockers. Escalate if stuck.

### 7. Delivery Done (0-14 days)
- **Entry**: All approvals received
- **Exit**: Live in production
- **Actions**: Final prep, launch planning, go-live coordination
- **Health check**: Should be quick — if stuck, something went wrong in review.

### 8. Launched
- **Entry**: Live in production
- **Actions**: Monitor, support, relationship management, expansion opportunities
- **Note**: This is an ongoing state, not a stage to "complete."

## Terminal States

### Blocked
Deal is stalled due to external factors.
- Always add a note explaining WHY it's blocked and what would unblock it.
- Review monthly — either unblock or move to Abandoned.

### Abandoned
Deal is dead.
- Add a post-mortem note: why it died, what we learned, any future angle.
- Don't delete — historical data is valuable.

### Archived Done
Completed deals moved to archive for clean pipeline view.

## Health Indicators

| Health | Meaning | Action |
|--------|---------|--------|
| On Track | Progressing normally | Continue |
| At Risk | Stalling or negative signals | Investigate, adjust approach |
| Off Track | Likely dead or major blockers | Escalate, consider abandoning |

## Velocity Benchmarks

- **Discovery (1→3)**: Target 30 days total
- **Delivery (4→7)**: Flag if 60+ days in one stage
- **No update in 14+ days**: Stale — needs attention
- **No update in 30+ days**: Likely dead — investigate immediately
