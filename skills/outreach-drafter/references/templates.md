# Outreach Templates

## Cold Email — Technical Decision Maker

**Subject**: {Specific hook: their product + your capability}

{Homework sentence — reference their specific product, architecture, or recent technical decision.}

{Your company} {one sentence: what you do that connects to their situation.}

{Concrete detail — specific capability, integration point, or metric that matters to THEM.}

{Specific ask: "Worth a 15-minute call to walk through how this works with {their product}?"}

{Name}

## Intro Request

**Subject**: Intro to {person} at {company}?

{Why specifically — the connection or angle.}

{Offer a forwardable blurb.}

**Forwardable**: "{2-3 sentences about your company and what you'd discuss.}"

---

## Rules

- Emails: <150 words
- DMs: <80 words
- Follow-ups: <100 words
- Always: specific opening (proves homework), one CTA, no buzzwords
- Subject lines: specific hooks, never generic
- Personalize with actual details from research, not generic value props
