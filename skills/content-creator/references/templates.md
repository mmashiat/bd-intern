## Partnership Proposal

### Structure
1. **Executive Summary** (3-4 sentences)
2. **About {Your Company}** (from config)
3. **About {Partner}** (from research)
4. **Proposed Collaboration** (specific scope, not vague)
5. **Technical Integration** (how products work together)
6. **Mutual Benefits** (both sides, quantified if possible)
7. **Next Steps** (specific actions + timeline)

### Rules
- 2-3 pages max
- Specific about what you'd build together
- Technical enough to be credible
- Business-focused enough for executives

## Case Study Outline

### Structure
1. **Challenge** (the problem, quantified)
2. **Solution** (how your product solved it)
3. **Implementation** (timeline, approach, key decisions)
4. **Results** (quantified outcomes, before/after)
5. **Quote** (placeholder for customer quote)

### Rules
- Lead with results in the title
- Specific numbers throughout
- Focus on the customer's perspective, not yours
